Shelldon's World

This big turtle is a resident of our local National Aquarium in the Inner Harbor of Baltimore City.
I don't know if he actually has a name, but Shelldon fits better anyway's. I picked Shelldon purely
because when I was looking for pictures it was looking right back at me and I thought it was funny.
Anyways Shelldon was actually really cool and surprisingly fast for a turtle.
